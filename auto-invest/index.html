Auto Invest - effortless Bitcoin investing

Auto Invest (ex. Invest Pro) is an exclusive investment opportunity on Plan B Lending, designed for those who want a simpler, automated way to invest in Bitcoin-backed loans starting at 150,000 EUR.

Unlike standard P2P lending, where investors manually select individual loan requests, Auto Invest uses Plan B Lending’s Instant Loans feature to seamlessly match your funds with borrowers through pre-collateralized loans, at an interest rate of your choice.

Get access to Auto-Invest

Fixed returns

Earn stable returns — set your rate and let Auto Invest match your funds. Current yields reach up to 10.9% APR in EUR and 8.9% APR in CZK.

Higher yields

Auto Invest funds are offered via Plan B Lending’s Instant Loans, delivering higher yields than standard P2P. Once matched, your funds earn interest for the full loan term.

Automatic funds allocation

Your funds are automatically allocated, eliminating the need to browse individual loan requests in the marketplace.

Security

Auto Invest offers the same security as individual P2P loans, powered by the non-custodial Plan B Lending protocol.

How it works in 6 simple steps

1. Investment request

Auto Invest is now open to everyone. The minimum investment is 150,000 EUR or 2,000,000 CZK.

2. Define interest rate

Pick the right rate to gain higher yields while minimizing waiting time before your funds start earning interest.

3. Legal agreement

Review and sign the legal agreement (template here) outlining the terms of your investment.

4. Funds deposit

Transfer the entire investment amount in a single payment to a dedicated bank account provided by Plan B Lending.

5. Automated loan matching

Plan B Lending SPV automatically allocates your funds to provide instant loans to borrowers, managing the entire process on your behalf.

6. Earn interest

As borrowers repay their loans, you receive the principal and fixed interest payments directly to your bank account.

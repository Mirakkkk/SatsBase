This is what we pledge to the people who use Firefish today…
…and to the ones who haven't found us yet.         

1

Bitcoin only. Deliberately.

Bitcoin is the only asset we work with. Not because we ignore everything else, but because we focus on what matters and we're not splitting our attention. Savings in bitcoin deserve tools built specifically for them. That's what we make.

2

An open market is the only honest one.

We bring Bitcoiners, investors and institutions together to transact on their own terms, secured by pristine collateral. Rates set by supply and demand, not by a desk in the middle. The market decides the price. We just built the venue.

3

Built in the open.

'Don't trust, verify' is not a slogan we borrowed, it is the engineering standard we hold ourselves to. The Plan B Lending protocol is open source and sits on GitHub. Read it, audit it, improve it. We welcome contributors as much as skeptics. Software that touches people's savings should be open to the people whose savings it touches.

4

Savings, not leverage.

Bitcoin is the store of value for the digital era. But stored value that cannot be accessed for productive use is only half useful. Collateralization is the natural extension, a way to put that value to work without surrendering the asset itself. That is what we build for. Tools for better savings, not for more leverage or speculation.

Hold us to it.

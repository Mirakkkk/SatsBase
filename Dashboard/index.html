What is Bitcoin-backed lending?
₿ → 💵
Bitcoin-backed lending lets you borrow cash without selling your BTC. You lock your Bitcoin as collateral in a secure escrow. A lender provides cash. You repay the loan + interest, and your BTC is returned.
Key benefit
You keep exposure to Bitcoin price appreciation. If BTC goes up while your loan is active, you benefit — your collateral is still yours after repayment.
What is collateral?
🔒
Collateral is the Bitcoin you lock up as security for the loan. It sits in a DLC escrow — neither you nor the lender can access it until the loan terms are resolved (repaid or liquidated).
Important to know
If you can't repay the loan, the lender can claim your collateral. Always borrow an amount you can repay.
Understanding LTV ratio
LTV (Loan-to-Value) = Loan amount ÷ Collateral value × 100
Below 60% — Safe zone. Buffer before liquidation.
60–75% — Warning zone. Consider adding collateral.
Above 80% — Liquidation triggers. BTC may be sold.
Plan B starts you at 50% LTV, giving you a 30% safety buffer before liquidation risk.
